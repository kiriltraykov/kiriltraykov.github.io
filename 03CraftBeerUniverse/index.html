Craft Beer in Brief

  • A craft brewery is a brewery that produces a small amount of beer
  • Craft breweries are independent and specialize in producing rarer beer styles
  • The Brewers Association reports that the average American lives within 10 miles of a craft brewery
  • The U.S. has more craft beer styles and brands to choose from than any other market in the world

Beer Ingredients

Water Yeast Malt Hops

Yeast can be of two types: Ale or Lager and feasts on sugar during fermentation and expels alcohol, carbon dioxide (CO2), and flavor compunds. The alcohol expelled gives the beer its Alcohol By Volume (ABV) percent.

Hops add bitterness to the beer. There is a standardized International Bitterness Units (IBU) scale that measures the bitterness of beer. It ranges from 0 to 100.

We will measure the craft beers by ABV and IBU!
